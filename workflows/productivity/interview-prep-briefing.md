# Interview Prep Briefing

> Generate one interview brief with company context, role fit, and your best stories before the call starts.

## What this is for

Use this before:

- recruiter screens
- hiring manager calls
- panel interviews
- final rounds

## Workflow

### 1. Collect the source material

Gather:

- job description
- company page
- your tailored resume
- any previous interview notes

### 2. Generate one prep brief

```text
Create an interview briefing from these materials.

Include:
- what the company seems to care about
- likely questions
- my strongest matching experiences
- gaps I should address honestly
- 5 questions I can ask them
```

### 3. Add your proof stories

For each likely question, attach:

- situation
- action
- result

Do this in your own words before the interview.

### 4. Review it once, not ten times

The brief should reduce panic, not create more tabs.

## Failure modes

- preparing generic answers with no examples
- no question list for the interviewer
- overloading the prep note with too much company trivia
