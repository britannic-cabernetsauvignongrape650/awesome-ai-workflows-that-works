# Music Production Automation

> Use AI where it saves time in music production: capture, cleanup, versioning, stem prep, mastering, and release packaging.

## What this is for

This is not a "press one button and the album is done" workflow.

It is for producers, songwriters, podcasters, and creator-musicians who want fewer boring steps between:

- the original idea
- the editable session
- the review link
- the final upload package

## Stack

| Tool | Role |
|------|------|
| Suno | song ideation, alternate versions, stems |
| DAW | arrangement, editing, automation, and final mix choices |
| Adobe Podcast or Descript | quick speech cleanup for voice notes, spoken intros, podcast sections, or rough vocals |
| BandLab or another mastering tool | fast comparison masters and shareable bounces |
| spreadsheet or project tracker | versions, credits, artwork status, and release checklist |

## Workflow

### 1. Keep one intake lane for ideas

Capture everything into one inbox:

- voice memos
- lyric notes
- reference links
- Suno drafts
- half-finished choruses

The mistake is not lack of ideas. It is scattering them across six apps.

### 2. Turn rough material into editable assets

Good conversions:

- voice note to lyric or arrangement note
- Suno draft to stems
- spoken intro to cleaned audio
- reference mix to checklist of what to change

### 3. Clean only the parts that need cleanup

Use AI cleanup selectively:

- spoken sections
- noisy voice memos
- tutorial audio
- quick demo explanations for collaborators

Do not flatten the life out of a full song mix just because a cleanup tool is available.

### 4. Standardize file naming before collaborators enter

Simple naming rule:

```text
project-name__bpm__key__version__date
```

This matters more than people admit. It cuts confusion when you are juggling multiple masters, stems, snippets, and artwork revisions.

### 5. Make two masters, not ten

For review:

- one balanced master
- one louder or brighter alternate if needed

For social:

- short preview export
- caption-ready vertical clip if relevant

### 6. Package the release

Before upload, confirm:

- title
- contributors
- lyrics
- cover art
- preview clip
- clean export folder

## Failure modes

- using AI cleanup on the entire mix
- no naming convention
- no version owner
- publishing a loud master that sounds worse everywhere else
