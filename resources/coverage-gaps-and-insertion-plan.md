# Coverage Gaps And Insertion Plan

This is the maintainer map for the next expansion wave.

As of March 28, 2026, the repo now covers coding agents, MCP, general productivity, students, job seekers, travel, and household coordination. The biggest remaining gaps are the communities that use AI every day without thinking of themselves as "AI builders", but in more sensitive or underserved areas.

## Highest-priority gaps

### Music producers and artist tools

Why it matters:

- large, loud community
- high sharing potential on GitHub, X, Discord, Reddit, and YouTube
- clear overlap with creators, podcasters, indie hackers, and agency teams

What to add next:

- `workflows/music/voice-note-to-demo.md`
- `workflows/music/ai-music-video-pipeline.md`
- `workflows/music/podcast-editing-and-chaptering.md`

- add a permanent `Music and audio` section in `README.md`

## Editorial fixes that still matter

These are not missing workflows, but they do affect growth:

- keep replacing weak source placeholders with direct links
- add screenshot-ready examples to the highest-traffic mainstream workflows
- keep the README balanced between technical and everyday use cases

## Rules for the next wave

- add communities, not just tools
- prefer small sharp workflows over giant encyclopedia pages
- keep consent, provenance, and disclosure explicit in synthetic-media workflows
- when a source is useful, absorb the pattern locally and cite it only where needed
